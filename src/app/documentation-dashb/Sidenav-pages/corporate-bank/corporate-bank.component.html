Business Banking

Enabling corporate ERP applications and solution to integrate through set of the Business Banking API offerings to facilitate - easy access to account information, facilitating transactions, seamlessly availing banking services, integration with internal systems, consolidation & transparency, financial data integration with vendors, suppliers and customers, channel and vendor financing. Integrated solutions can be created for businesses whether merchants, SMEs or large businesses to enable consolidated access to information on their accounts and transactions. The information services facilitate seamless access of this information to your legacy or bespoke systems based on authentication. The APIs can also provide great visibility and transparency across your supply chain to equip your business and enable better tracking. Better and up-to-date information facilitates sound & prompt decision making.

Account opening

Business category

Account opening API enables customer to apply for a current account directly from partner's platform.

Corporate Internet Banking

Business category

Corporate Internet Banking APIs enable the customer/partner to access ICICI Bank's internet portal services on his platform, like payment, bank statement, balance fetch and can apply for instant overdraft.

Cash Deposit Machine

Business category

Cash Deposit Machine APIs enable clients to authenticate the cash depositor in Cash Deposit Machine and update the deposited amount to the client's server.

CMS Payments

Business category

CMS Payments API enable the clients to upload payment file containing multiple transactions through NEFT; RTGS; IMPS; and FT. The client can also get transaction status through the API.

CMS Collection

Business category

CMS Collection facilitates clients to authenticate payer details and get credit information for recurring inward RTGS, NEFT , IMPS, Cash, Cheque, and DD in their account.

IMPS API

Business category

IMPS API enables client to make payment through IMPS mode. Client can also get the transaction status through API.
