Building Blocks

The Building Blocks APIs are a set of common APIs which address ancillary requirements for integration to various processes. These set of APIs provide capabilities like retrieve credit rating scores, validation services to establish authenticity, eligibility checks, list of available Pre approved offers and multiple other common capabilities. These can be integrated with ease into your API sequence and be useful in the execution of the multiple business use case across various processes, products and solutions.

Pre approved offers

Building Blocks category

ICICI Bank prepares various product oriented Pre approved offers for its existing customer in all the domains -Cards, Loans, Payments etc. These APIs allows you to directly access these Pre approved offers and present it to your customers without any hassle. We provide you each step wise integration to present these Pre approved offers to your customers and avail them without even visiting any branch, Relationship Manager or even our website.

Customer Authentication

Building Blocks category

Leverage these APIs for customer authentication based on mobile number or customer account etc.

New Customer Onboarding

Building Blocks category

In day-to-day working, most of the times Businesses need to open bank accounts for either their customers or their employees, these APIs allow you to open a bank account for an individual without making them to visit any branch. With various modular products you can decide what facilities you want to provide to your customer, ranging from Face Scan APIs to Credit Card Ordering APIs.
