Loans And Cards

APIs related to Credit Cards, Pre-paid cards and loan products facilitate the end to end seamless customer journey, of initiating customer request, customer onboarding journey and lifecycle management for the respective products. Create innovative solutions for customers leveraging the APIs for personal, car or housing and other loans. Some of the key offerings:

Personal Loans APIs

Loans category

A suite of APIs covering various stages of the lifecycle of personal loans from checking eligibility, applying, to facilitate disbursement and view current status/dues.

Auto Loans APIs

Loans category

A suite of APIs covering various stages of the lifecycle of automobile loans from checking eligibility, sanctioning, applying to facilitate disbursement and view current status/dues

Loan Top-Up

Loans category

Enabling innovative solution to enhance and ease the customer journey for top-up loans for mortagage and automobile loans, through a set of APIs supporting the end to end processes.

Loan Management

Loans category

A set of APIs that support loans management for customers through statements , transaction details , payment schedules , payments , closure and other services.

Credit Cards

Loans category

Credit Cards API support the end to end customer journey, from initiating the customer request for credit cards, available cards based on customer profile , enabling request processing , credit card creation and services to manage credit cards.

Prepaid Cards

Loans category

Explore the products like - Expressions Gift Card - a perfect instant gift bundled with personalized messages for every occasion. PayDirect Card- The PayDirect Card is a magnetic-strip-based reloadable Prepaid Card that addresses the concerns of an enterprise for payment of salaries, reimbursements, commissions and incentives to its employees/associates. With the API Suite of Pre-Paid Cards, the customers can easily create and manage all the prepaid cards.

Paylater

Loans category

PayLater is a digital credit product, similar to a Credit Card. With it you can make your purchases today and make payments within 45 days of the transaction date and no interest is charged. The API integration allows you to facilitate your customers with the PayLater product on your platform.
